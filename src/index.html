about the event

Robotics workshop and Championship is a national level championship organized by GNA University in association with Robosapiens Technologies Pvt. Ltd. which is India’s leading Robotics Academy.
Budding engineers will learn the skill of making the robots and will compete in the championship on the next day. This is the opportunity to come forward, learn, compete and win the championship. It’s a two day event. First day is dedicated to the learning of the skill of designing, programming and making robots and on the second day all the participants will participate in the Robotics competition.
